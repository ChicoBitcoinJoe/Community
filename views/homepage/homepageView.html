Community Homepage (pre-alpha)

Please make sure you have...

  • Exposed the web3 object to your browser (Metamask,Mist,Geth)
  • A running ipfs daemon

Community does some things differently!

  • There are two voting systems: public (viewable by anyone) and private (this data stays client side and never reaches the web)
  • Usernames are hidden until you cast a private vote on that specific comment
  • Any public action will cost around 1 cent. This includes: posts,comments, setting a username, and casting a public vote
  • Posts are sorted by their combined score which is determined by a voting algorithm
  • Posts with a combined score of less than 20 will be hidden. This number will likely evolve over time as better voting algorithms are discovered

In the event something goes wrong...

  • Clearing local storage can help if data got corrupted
  • Hard Refresh the page (ctrl+shift+r)
